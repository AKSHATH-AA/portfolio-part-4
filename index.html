Character review

Shadow The hedgehog is a very popular character because he is super fast and cool. I think that he is the perfect villain in the series and he provides just enough awesomeness. For more information about shadow got to the about section. He is serious (most of the time), cold, calculating, ruthless, and a tad egotistical. He will do anything to get what he wants, which makes him rather impulsive at times. He doesn't have much respect for Sonic or anyone else for that matter and often says things that hurt whoever he's talking to.
